Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How often does the parking data refresh?

A: Our system provides true real-time data. Status changes (Occupied/Available) are detected and updated on the Live Map via API within **500 milliseconds** of the event occurring at the sensor level.

Q: What is the maintenance schedule for the ground sensors?

A: The sensors are designed to be maintenance-free for 5+ years due to their low-power consumption. We perform over-the-air diagnostics daily, and physical checks are typically only required once every 2 years or upon battery life expiry.

Q: Can the system integrate with existing city parking meters?

A: Yes. Our cloud platform uses an open API structure and is designed to integrate seamlessly with most legacy payment systems and enforcement software via standard REST APIs or custom middleware solutions.

Q: Is the system secure from cyber threats?

A: Data transmission from the sensor to the cloud is encrypted using industry-standard protocols (e.g., AES-128). Our cloud infrastructure adheres to SOC 2 Type II compliance standards for data integrity and security.
